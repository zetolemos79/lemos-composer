About

Lemos is a Portuguese pianist, Yamaha Artist and composer working across film scoring, trailer music and functional wellness sound.

His scores have been used in feature film and documentary productions released internationally, and his wellness catalogue has generated over 100 million streams across major DSP platforms.

His work combines cinematic precision, harmonic clarity and emotional architecture — allowing his music to function both in narrative film environments and large-scale streaming ecosystems.
